Byteint Localspaces

Byteint LocalSpaces, as the name suggests, run on your personal computer. They provide a custom domain "byteint.replit.app/yourprojectname/pagelocation". You just need to run the code, type in the port number on the Byteint app, and Byteint will connect the app running on your machine to the custom domain.

Install
